Key Highlights of TCS NQT Exam 2025

Exam Structure

The test comprises 5 sections:

Topic No. of Questions Time (Minutes)
Verbal Ability 24 30
Reasoning Ability 30 50
Numerical Ability 26 40
Programming Logic 10 15
Hands-on Coding 2 45

Total Questions

The paper includes 92 questions, covering all sections.

Exam Duration

Candidates will have 180 minutes to complete the test.

Preparation Tips

  • Ensure you cover the entire syllabus topic by topic.
  • Practice TCS NQT PYPs and solve sample questions regularly.
  • Focus on improving your time management skills during mock tests.
