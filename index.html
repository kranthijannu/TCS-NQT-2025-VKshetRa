About TCS NQT

The TCS National Qualifier Test (NQT) is a prestigious assessment conducted by Tata Consultancy Services to evaluate the skills of aspiring professionals in areas such as Aptitude, Verbal Ability, and Logical Reasoning. It serves as a gateway to employment opportunities in TCS and other top companies.

Benefits of taking the TCS NQT:

  • Opens doors to career opportunities with TCS and other reputed companies.
  • Enhances problem-solving and analytical abilities.
  • Gives a competitive edge in campus placements and hiring drives.
  • Helps build confidence through industry-recognized certification.

Important Dates for TCS NQT 2025

  • Registration Deadline (March Cycle): 19th March 2025
  • Exam Date (March Cycle): 31st March 2025

To perform well in the TCS NQT exam, it is essential to understand the complete syllabus, practice previous year papers (PYPs), sample papers, and mock tests. This will help strengthen your preparation and improve your confidence for the exam.
